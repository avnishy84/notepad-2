    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is One Editor?

    +

    One Editor is a simple, fast, and secure note-taking app designed for modern productivity. It allows you to create, edit, and organize your notes with ease, featuring rich text editing, keyboard shortcuts, and even a built-in Snake game for breaks.

    Is One Editor free to use?

    +

    Yes, One Editor is completely free to use. You can create an account and start taking notes right away with all features included.

    How do I save my notes?

    +

    Your notes are automatically saved to the cloud when you are logged in. You can also download your notes as HTML files for backup or sharing with others.

    What keyboard shortcuts are available?

    +

    One Editor supports many keyboard shortcuts including Ctrl+B for bold, Ctrl+I for italic, Ctrl+N for new note, Ctrl+S for save, Ctrl+D for download, Ctrl+G for Snake game, and Ctrl+M for dark mode toggle.

    How do I play the Snake game?

    +

    Click the 🎮 button in the header or press Ctrl+G to open the Snake game. Use arrow keys or WASD to control the snake, eat food to grow, and avoid hitting walls or yourself. The game window is draggable for comfortable positioning.

    Can I use Google Sign-In?

    +

    Yes! You can sign up or log in using your Google account for quick and secure access. Just click the "Continue with Google" button in the login modal.

    Is my data secure?

    +

    Absolutely! One Editor uses Firebase Authentication and Firestore for secure data storage. Your notes are encrypted and only accessible to you when logged in.
